What to Expect on Your Desert Safari

Our desert safaris begin with complimentary hotel pickup in our air-conditioned vehicles. After approximately 45 minutes, we arrive at the desert where the adventure begins with dune bashing in our modern 4x4 vehicles. Following the thrilling ride, you'll have the opportunity to try various desert activities before heading to our traditional Bedouin-style camp for dinner and entertainment.

Best Time for Desert Safari in Dubai

The ideal time for desert safari in Dubai is from October to April when temperatures are milder. During summer months (May to September), we recommend morning or overnight safaris to avoid the extreme afternoon heat.
